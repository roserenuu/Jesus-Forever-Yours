## 2. Key Viral Trends & Insights

### A. The "CTA-First" Hook
Many top creators are now integrating their Call to Action (CTA) directly into the hook or very early in the video. 
- **Pattern**: "If this message is for you, comment [KEYWORD]..."
- **Why it works**: It filters for high-intent viewers immediately and boosts the comment count, which tells the algorithm the content is engaging.

### B. Aesthetic B-Roll vs. Talking Head
While talking head videos build trust, **Aesthetic B-roll** (scenery, reading a book, cozy settings) with a powerful voiceover or text overlay is seeing a massive spike in "Saves" and "Shares."
- **Action**: For @jesusforeveryours, prioritize these aesthetic "mood" videos to increase shareability.

### C. Podcast-Style Authority
Creators like @georgejanko and @girlsgonebible use a "podcast" or "interview" visual setup. This creates an immediate sense of authority and wisdom.
- **Action**: Try a "Micro-Podcast" style Reel for @roserenuu where you're speaking into a high-quality mic (as you already do) but with a slightly more "interview" or "candid" camera angle.
