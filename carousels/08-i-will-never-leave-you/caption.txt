I Will Never Leave You

People leave. Seasons change. Life shifts in ways you never expected. But there is one thing that will never change. God will never leave you. He is not like the people who walked away. He is not like the ones who gave up on you. He does not love you when it is convenient and disappear when it gets hard. His presence is permanent. His love is unconditional. His commitment to you is unbreakable. So even when everyone else walks out, He walks in. Even when you feel completely alone, He is right there. You have never been abandoned by God. And you never will be.
