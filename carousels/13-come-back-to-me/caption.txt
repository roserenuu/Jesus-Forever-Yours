Come Back To Me

If you have been running from God, He is not chasing you with anger. He is chasing you with love. He is not keeping a record of how far you have gone or how long you have been away. He is just waiting for you to turn around. And the moment you do, He will be right there. Arms wide open. No judgment. No lecture. No conditions. Just love. It does not matter what you have done. It does not matter how many times you have fallen. God is not done with you. He never gave up on you. And He never will. So come back to Him. He has been waiting for you this whole time.
