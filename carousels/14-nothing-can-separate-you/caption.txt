Nothing Can Separate You

Nothing can separate you from the love of God. Not your past. Not your mistakes. Not the things you are ashamed of. Not the darkest night you have ever walked through. His love is not something you have to earn back. It was yours before you were born and it will be yours forever. No matter what happens, no matter where you go, no matter how far you feel, He will always love you. That is a promise that will never change.
