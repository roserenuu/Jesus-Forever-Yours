CAROUSEL 4 - You Are Enough (dusty rose/mauve)

You Are Enough

You have been measuring yourself by the wrong standard. Comparing yourself to people who do not carry your calling. Beating yourself up for not being further along. But God is not disappointed in you. He is proud of you. You are enough. Not because of what you have done but because of who made you. He did not create you to be anyone else. He created you to be you. And you, exactly as you are right now, are enough for Him. Stop striving for approval you already have. Stop chasing a version of yourself that God never asked for. You are His masterpiece. And masterpieces do not need to prove their worth.
