I Chose You Before You Chose Me

Before you ever knew His name, He already knew yours. Before you ever reached for Him, He was already reaching for you. God did not wait for you to be perfect. He did not wait for you to have it all together. He chose you in your mess. He chose you in your doubt. He chose you before you even had the chance to choose Him back. That is not human love. That is God love. You were never an afterthought. You were always the plan. So if you have been wondering whether you matter, whether you belong, whether you are wanted, hear this. You were chosen on purpose, with purpose, by the God of the universe. And He would choose you again.
