I Know Your Heart

You do not have to explain yourself to God. He already knows. He knows the things you are too afraid to say out loud. He knows the prayers you have been praying in silence. He knows the battles you fight when no one is watching. He knows your heart better than anyone ever will. And He loves every part of it. The messy parts. The broken parts. The parts you try to hide from the world. He sees it all and He still chooses you. You do not have to perform for God. You do not have to pretend. Just come as you are. He already knows your heart and He loves what He sees.
