My Grace Is Enough

You keep thinking you need to be stronger. You keep telling yourself that you should have it all figured out by now. But God is not asking you to be perfect. He is asking you to let His grace be enough. His grace covers every mistake you have made. His grace fills every gap you cannot fill on your own. His grace meets you in your weakest moment and says I am still here. You do not have to earn it. You do not have to deserve it. It is a gift. And it is more than enough for whatever you are facing today. Stop trying to be enough on your own. His grace already is.
