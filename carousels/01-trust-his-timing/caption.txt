Trust His Timing

God's timing is not late. It is not early. It is perfect. If you have been waiting for something and it feels like nothing is happening, hear this. God has not forgotten about you. He is not ignoring your prayers. He is working behind the scenes in ways you cannot see yet. The waiting is not wasted. It is preparation. Every delay has a purpose. Every closed door is protection. What God has for you will not pass you by. So take a deep breath. Release the need to control the timeline. Trust the one who holds time in His hands. He is making all things beautiful in His time.
