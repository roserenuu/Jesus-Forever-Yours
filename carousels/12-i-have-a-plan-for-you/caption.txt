I Have A Plan For You

You may not see it yet but God has a plan for your life. A plan that was written before you took your first breath. A plan that includes every detour, every delay, every disappointment. Nothing has been wasted. Nothing has been random. Every single thing you have been through is part of a bigger picture that only God can see. And it is beautiful. So when life does not make sense, when the road takes an unexpected turn, when you feel lost and confused, remember this. God is not lost. He knows exactly where He is taking you. And where He is taking you is better than where you have been.
