Do Not Be Afraid

Fear has been lying to you. It has been telling you that you are not safe, that things will fall apart, that the worst is coming. But God says do not be afraid. Not because bad things will never happen. But because He will be with you through every single one of them. You are not walking into the unknown alone. He goes before you. He stands beside you. He catches you when you fall. The fear you feel is real but it is not the truth. The truth is that the God who holds the universe in His hands is holding you too. And He is not letting go.
