Rest In Me

You were not created to carry everything on your own. You were not designed to run without stopping, to give without receiving, to push through every season without rest. God is not asking you to do more. He is asking you to come to Him. To lay it all down. To breathe. Rest is not weakness. It is worship. It is trusting that God can handle what you cannot. It is believing that the world will not fall apart if you take a moment to be still. So stop running. Stop striving. Stop trying to hold it all together. Come to Him and rest. He has been waiting for you.
