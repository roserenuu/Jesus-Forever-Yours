I Am Fighting For You

You may feel like you are fighting alone but you are not. God is fighting for you right now. In the battles you can see and the ones you cannot. He has not stepped back. He has not looked away. He is standing between you and everything that is trying to break you. The enemy wants you to believe you are on your own. That is a lie. God is your defender, your protector, your shield. You do not have to win this fight by yourself. Let Him take the lead. Let Him carry what is too heavy. The battle belongs to the Lord and He has already won it for you.
