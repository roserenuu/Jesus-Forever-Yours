Let Go Of What Hurt You

You have been holding on to something that was never meant to stay. The pain, the betrayal, the words that cut deep. You keep replaying it hoping it will make sense. But it will not. Some things were never meant to be understood. They were meant to be released. God is asking you to let it go. Not because it did not matter. But because it is blocking what He has for you next. You cannot receive new blessings with hands that are still gripping old pain. Release what hurts you so it does not harm what God is giving you. He has so much more ahead. But you have to open your hands first.
