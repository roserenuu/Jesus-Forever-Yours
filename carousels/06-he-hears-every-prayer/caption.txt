He Hears Every Prayer

Every prayer you have whispered in the dark, He heard it. Every cry you thought went unanswered, He caught it. God is not silent. He is working. Just because you have not seen the answer yet does not mean He is not moving. Sometimes God's silence is not absence. It is preparation. He is aligning things you cannot see. He is protecting you from things you do not know about. He is making sure the answer comes at the exact right time. So do not stop praying. Do not stop believing. Your prayers are not hitting the ceiling. They are reaching the throne of God. And He is listening to every single one.
