Shipping Policy

Last updated March 2026

Thank you for your order. Here is everything you need to know about shipping and delivery for Forever Yours.

Digital Orders

Digital PDF orders are delivered instantly to your email address immediately after purchase. Please check your spam folder if you do not receive your download link within a few minutes. For any issues with your digital order please contact us at support@jesusforeveryours.com.

Hardcover Orders

Hardcover books are printed on demand and fulfilled through IngramSpark. Please allow 4 to 6 weeks from the date of your order for delivery. You will receive a confirmation email with tracking information once your order has shipped.

Shipping Destinations

We ship hardcover orders worldwide. Shipping times may vary depending on your location. International orders may be subject to customs fees or import duties which are the responsibility of the recipient.

Order Issues

If your order arrives damaged or you have not received it within 6 weeks please contact us at support@jesusforeveryours.com and we will do our best to make it right.

Returns

Because our hardcover books are printed on demand we are unable to accept returns unless the item arrives damaged or defective. Digital purchases are non-refundable once delivered.

Contact

Questions about your order? Email us at support@jesusforeveryours.com and we will get back to you within 2 to 3 business days.
