About the Book

Hear God speak directly to your heart

Forever Yours: 24 Days With Jesus is a 24-day devotional written as intimate love letters from God. Each day opens with "My Child" and closes with "Forever Yours, Heavenly Father" — reminding you that no matter what season you are walking through, you are never alone.

Each letter is paired with scripture and speaks to the deepest parts of the human heart: faith, healing, peace, love, and new beginnings.

A Letter For You — Day 12

The Peace You Need

My Child,

I see the weight you have been carrying. The worry that finds you in the quiet of the night, the questions that seem to have no answers, the ache of things that feel too heavy to hold.

Bring Me what you have been carrying. I will trade it for My peace — a peace that does not depend on your circumstances, that cannot be shaken by the storms of this world. It is already yours. You only have to receive it.

You were never meant to carry this alone. I am here, and I have always been here.

Forever Yours,
Heavenly Father
"Peace I leave with you; my peace I give you. I do not give to you as the world gives." — John 14:27

Meet the Author

Rose Renuu

Rose Renuu is a faith-based content creator whose voice has reached hundreds of thousands across Instagram, TikTok, Facebook, and YouTube. Her content — intimate, encouraging love letters written as though from God — has become a source of comfort and renewal for people in every kind of season.

Her debut devotional, Forever Yours: 24 Days With Jesus, was born from years of writing letters she wished someone had handed her in her hardest moments. Available in hardcover and eBook, it is written for anyone who has ever needed to hear that they are not alone.
