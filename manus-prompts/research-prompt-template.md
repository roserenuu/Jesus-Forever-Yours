# Manus Research Prompt Template

Use these prompts when asking Manus to research content creators.
Save results to the matching subfolder in research/.

---

## Instagram Research Prompt

Research the top performing Christian faith content creators on Instagram right now.
Focus on creators in the encouraging/devotional/love letters from God niche.

For each creator provide:
- Username
- Follower count
- Content style (what they post, format, tone)
- What is performing best for them (saves, shares, comments)
- Hook styles they use
- Posting frequency
- What I (@roserenuu / @jesusforeveryours) can learn or adapt

Save results to: research/instagram/[date]-instagram-research.md

---

## YouTube Research Prompt

Research the top performing Christian faith content creators on YouTube right now.
Focus on creators doing devotionals, scripture readings, love letters from God, or encouraging faith content.

For each creator provide:
- Channel name
- Subscriber count
- Video formats that perform best (Shorts vs long form, thumbnails, hooks)
- Topics getting the most views
- What I (@roserenuu) can adapt for my YouTube channel

Save results to: research/youtube/[date]-youtube-research.md
