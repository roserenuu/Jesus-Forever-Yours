## Platform-Specific Notes

**TikTok**
- Same video content as Instagram Reels works
- Trending sounds can boost reach — check research/tiktok/

**Facebook**
- Older audience, responds well to longer captions
- Can share Instagram posts directly

**YouTube**
- Shorts: same reel videos work
- Long form: consider devotional-style reading videos (could expand reel scripts)
