7. Each letter uses "God is telling you today" as the hook line placed after the scripture and before "My Child." Do NOT use "The Lord is reminding you today" line.
8. Scripture translations used: NLT, NIV, EASY, ERV, CEV, MSG, KJV, TLB (variety is good).
9. No emojis ever.
10. These can be used for carousels on @jesusforeveryours, standalone posts, or as source material for reel scripts.
