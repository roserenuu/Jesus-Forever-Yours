Daily Devotional

Draw closer to
Jesus every day.

Excerpts from Forever Yours paired with Scripture and reflection. Let these words remind you how deeply and personally God loves you.

Day 1 | God's Faithfulness

Before you ever called My name, I was already there.

My beloved,

Before you took your first breath, I knew every detail of your life. Every joy, every tear, every silent prayer. I have never once looked away from you. Even when you could not feel Me, I was holding you. I am faithful, even when the world is not.

You were never an accident. You were never forgotten. You were chosen, wanted, and loved before time began. Let that truth settle deep in your heart today.

Forever Yours, Jesus

"I have loved you with an everlasting love; I have drawn you with unfailing kindness."

Jeremiah 31:3

Reflect

When was the last time you truly felt God's presence? Take a moment today to sit quietly and ask Him to remind you of His faithfulness. Write down one way He has shown up for you, even when you did not expect it.

Day 5 | Healing

I see the wounds you carry.

My child,

I know the pain you have been holding. The things you do not say out loud, the ache that wakes you in the night. I see it all. And I want you to know that your brokenness does not scare Me.

Bring your wounds to Me. Not when you have cleaned them up, not when you feel worthy. Bring them now. My hands are gentle, and My love is the salve that heals what nothing else can reach.

Forever Yours, Jesus

"He heals the brokenhearted and binds up their wounds."

Psalm 147:3

Reflect

What wound have you been trying to heal on your own? Today, surrender it to God in prayer. Write it down and then release it. He is already at work in the places you cannot see.

Day 12 | Set Free

The chains you feel have already been broken.

My beloved,

That thing that has held you captive for so long, that shame, that habit, that fear. I already defeated it. The chains were broken at the cross. You just have to let go.

Freedom is not something you earn. It is something I give. Stop trying to fight your way out of what I have already delivered you from. Walk forward in the freedom that is already yours.

Forever Yours, Jesus

"So if the Son sets you free, you will be free indeed."

John 8:36

Reflect

What are you still carrying that Jesus has already paid for? Name it out loud today. Declare your freedom in His name and take one step forward, trusting that His grace is more than enough.

Day 24 | Forever Yours

I came down to show you the way.

My beloved,

I came down to earth to show you the way. Not to watch from a distance, but to walk with you, to feel what you feel, and to carry what you could not carry.

This is not the end of our story. It is only the beginning. Every morning, I will be here. Every night, I will be near. There is no distance that can separate you from My love. You are mine, and I am yours. Forever.

Forever Yours, Jesus

"For I am convinced that neither death nor life, neither angels nor demons, neither the present nor the future, nor any powers, neither height nor depth, nor anything else in all creation, will be able to separate us from the love of God."

Romans 8:38-39

Reflect

As you close this 24 day journey, write a letter back to God. Tell Him what these days have meant to you. Thank Him for the ways He has shown up. This is not a goodbye. It is a new beginning.
