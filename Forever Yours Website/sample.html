Forever With You

My Child,

I am with you in every moment and every chapter of your life. When happiness fills your heart, I am your God of blessings. When you are broken by pain, I am your God of healing. When you are wronged, I am your God of justice. When you are overwhelmed by fear, I am your God of courage. When guilt weighs heavily on you, I am your God of forgiveness.

When shame makes you feel unworthy, I am your God of grace. When you see no way forward, I am your God of miracles. I am the same yesterday, today, and forever. You will never walk through a struggle or a joyful moment without Me by your side. In every season, I am your God.

Paid By Love

My Child,

I came down to earth to show you the way. Not to watch from a distance, but to walk with you, to feel what you feel, and to carry what you could not carry. I gave My life not because you had to prove your worth, but because I love you. Even in your broken places, I saw beauty. In your wandering, I saw someone worth rescuing. I came for you without hesitation. My love is stronger than the darkness you have faced. My mercy is more powerful than your past.

You no longer need to strive to be enough or make sacrifices to reach Me. I became the sacrifice. I took your place so you can take My hand. I defeated death and rose again, so you can rise into new life with Me. You rise above shame, above fear, above the grip of sin. You rise into purpose, into identity, and into the fullness of being My beloved child.
