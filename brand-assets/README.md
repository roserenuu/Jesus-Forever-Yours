# Brand Assets

Place your brand asset files in this folder.

Required files:
- `vector.png` — Forever Yours logo (PNG)
- `vector_01.png` — Forever Yours logo alternate (PNG)
- `vector.pdf` — Forever Yours logo (PDF)
- `book_cover.png` — Forever Yours: 24 Days With Jesus book cover

These files are referenced in carousel slides and brand content.
Export from Manus or Canva and drop them here.

## Book Mocks (`book-mocks/`)

Place Forever Yours book mockup images here:
- `front-cover-mock.png` — Front cover standing alone
- `front-back-mock.png` — Front and back cover side by side
- `back-cover-mock.png` — Back cover standing alone
